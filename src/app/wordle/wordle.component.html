WORDLE

Rules

  • The game generates a secret word consisting of 5 unique letters.
  • The player must guess the secret word by entering a 5-letter word.
  • After each guess, the game provides feedback on how many letters are correct:
    • A letter in green indicates that it is in the correct position in the secret word.
    • A letter in orange indicates that it is in the secret word, but in a different position.
    • A letter in gray indicates that it is not in the secret word at all.
    • A letter in red indicates that it is not a valid word.
  • The player has 6 attempts to guess the secret word.
  • If the player correctly guesses the secret word within 6 attempts, they win the game. Otherwise, they lose.
